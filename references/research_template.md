# Research Template

Use this structure for a Serenity-style high-quality thesis:

1. Starting Trend
   - AI infrastructure, CPO, HBM, power, robotics, Physical AI, or another large demand wave.

2. Supply-Chain Position
   - Where the company sits: material, component, equipment, testing, module, system, customer, or infrastructure.

3. Chokepoint
   - What becomes scarce, hard to qualify, hard to substitute, or under-covered.

4. Why Market May Miss It
   - Small cap, weak coverage, obscure technology, timing uncertainty, hidden operating leverage, or misunderstood customer exposure.

5. Evidence
   - Earnings, orders, capex, technical documents, patents, customer commentary, price/volume, or peer read-throughs.

6. Catalyst
   - Product ramp, design win, customer qualification, capacity shortage, earnings inflection, or industry event.

7. Risks
   - Liquidity, hype, Serenity's own influence, customer concentration, dilution, substitution, delayed adoption, or valuation.

8. Tracking Indicators
   - Backlog, gross margin, capex, qualification progress, customer wins, technical milestones, and follow-up revisions.
