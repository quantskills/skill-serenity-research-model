# Review Rules

## Keep

Keep rows that show Serenity's own forward-looking thesis with:

- ticker or clear asset
- theme
- supply-chain position
- chokepoint or mechanism
- evidence
- catalyst or time horizon
- risk or invalidation

## Deweight

Deweight rows that are:

- broad watchlists
- thematic baskets
- incomplete DD
- event explainers
- low-conviction comments
- missing risk or evidence

## Remove From Forward Signal

Remove rows from forward-signal analysis when they are:

- quote-only
- reply-only
- retrospective performance recap
- post-event explanation
- sarcasm, joke, or disclaimer-heavy content
- ticker appearing only as comparison or quoted text

## Delete

Delete duplicate, irrelevant, private, unauthorized, or malformed rows.

## Weighting Principle

The highest-quality Serenity signals are not merely ticker mentions. They combine a large trend, specific supply-chain role, chokepoint, evidence, catalyst, and risk.
